The exact steps

How to Start This Week — Step by Step

1

Pick ONE service to offer

Don't try to do everything. Pick automation, content writing, or chatbot building. Master one, then expand. Most people fail by spreading too thin.

2

Learn the tool in 1 week

Zapier, Make.com, and Claude all have free plans and YouTube tutorials. Spend 1–2 hours a day for a week. You'll know enough to help real clients.

3

Build one free demo project

Create a sample automation or piece of content for a fake business. Record a short screen video showing it working. This is your portfolio.

4

Reach out to 10 local businesses

Instagram DM or LinkedIn message. Keep it simple: "I noticed you're doing X manually — I can automate that. Want a free 10-minute demo?" Expect 1–2 responses. That's enough.

5

Charge for your second project

Offer the first client a discount. Charge the second client full price. Once you have one testimonial, everything becomes easier.
